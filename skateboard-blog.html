Electric Skateboarding

If you’ve never ridden a skateboard, then good. Don’t. Technology has evolved, and so must we. Electricity has granted us the power of instant torque, and use it we must!

An electric skateboard is, well, just that. A skateboard powered by electricity. Now, while this might sound like a somewhat mild idea, it’s the closest we can get to a hoverboard without building a time machine.

I got my board from Evolve skateboards a year ago, and since then I’ve had a blast. I opted for the offroad version, which takes away some of the top speed and distance for the ability to go wherever you’d like. Boy am I glad I did, as I’ve gone on to ride ovals, mountains, hiking trails, and most bike trails on the central coast.

There’s nothing quite like that feeling of power you get when accelerating from a standstill, or out of a tight corner. It’s like being pulled into another dimension, one of force and motion, one that grips your heart and doesn’t let it go until you let off that trigger.
