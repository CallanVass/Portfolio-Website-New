The Building Of This Website

Initially, this website looked like the image you see above. I really wanted to believe it looked good, and I truly believe that if I’d progressed with it I would have achieved a passing grade. After all, it had some pretty decent media queries contained within, and the colour wasn’t that bad, was it?

This kept nagging at me for about a day. I’d already spent about 15 hours on the thing, and that was just getting the home page right! Eventually, the intrusive thoughts won and I thought to myself if I was going to do it, I wanted to do it right. After all, this was something potential employers were going to see!

So like the good boy I am, I scraped the entire thing and set my sights on something greater and more colourful than I could ever have imagined. A shout out to Alicia Han (a past Coder Academy student) for inspiring me to go after a nicer design.

So here we are, winning the battle with mediocrity. Do you like the site? My favourite part is probably the colours and the background imagery. Even now just writing this it's easy to see how much I’ve learned in the past few days alone.
